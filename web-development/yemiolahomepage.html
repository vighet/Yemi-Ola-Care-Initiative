Our Mission

We provide support and resources to vulnerable individuals and communities, promoting dignity, hope and positive change.

Program Overview

YEMI-OLA Care Initiative aims to address the needs of underserved populations, including the elderly, children, and families in crisis. Our program focuses on providing:

  • Essential care packages (food, clothing, toileteries)
  • Emotional support and counseling
  • Access to healthcare and educational resources
  • Community development initiatives
